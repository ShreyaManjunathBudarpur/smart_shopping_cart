Access Smart Cart on Your Mobile Device

How to access from your phone:

  1. Open your Replit project in your browser
  2. Click the "Share" button at the top of the Replit interface
  3. Copy the link that looks like: https://your-project-name.username.replit.dev
  4. Open that URL on your mobile device
  5. Add /welcome to the end of the URL (this is important!)
  6. Your final URL should look like: https://your-project-name.username.replit.dev/welcome

Note: The URL that starts with "localhost" won't work on your phone because "localhost" only refers to the device you're currently using.

You need to use the public Replit URL to access the Smart Cart app from your mobile device.
